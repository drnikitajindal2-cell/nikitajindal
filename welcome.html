About Dr. Nikita Jindal

Dr. Nikita Jindal, MD, DNB

Pediatrician

With over 7 years of experience in pediatric medicine, Dr. Nikita Jindal is dedicated to providing comprehensive care for children of all ages including newborns.

Dr Nikita completed her medical degree from Maulana Azad Medical College. She has a rationale approach to all cases, stays current with the latest advancements in pediatric medicine and does not encourage unnecessary investigations.

Dr Nikita believes in building strong relationships with her patients and their families, taking the time to listen to concerns and explain conditions and treatments in understandable terms.

  • MBBS, Murshidabad Medical College & Hospital
  • MD Paediatrics, Maulana Azad Medical College, New Delhi
  • DNB Paediatrics, National Board of Examination
  • Paediatric Senior Residency, Maulana Azad Medical College & associated Lok Nayak Hospital
  • 7+ years of experience in neonatology and paediatric patient care
